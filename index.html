A fantastic place to relax and read. The library stores all sorts of books: from adventure books to cooking books.



School Auditorium

Was established in 1978

Our auditorium is used for displaying plays as well as doing indoor sport such as hockey, netball,table tennis and more. As well as this we use the audotorium to hold our weekly assemblies.
